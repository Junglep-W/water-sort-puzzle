How to Play Water Sort Puzzle

Game Objective

Water Sort Puzzle is a brain teaser where you need to sort colored water in bottles until each bottle contains only one color. The game starts with several bottles partially filled with different colored water layers. Your goal is to rearrange these colored layers by pouring water from one bottle to another until all colors are properly sorted.

Game Rules

  • You can only pour water if the receiving bottle has enough space
  • You can only pour water if the top color of the pouring bottle matches the top color of the receiving bottle (or if the receiving bottle is empty)
  • Once you start pouring, you must pour all connected layers of the same color
  • The puzzle is solved when each bottle contains either one color only or is completely empty

Tips & Tricks

Strategy Guide

  • Plan ahead: Before making your first move, analyze the bottles and think several steps ahead.
  • Clear empty bottles first: Having empty bottles gives you more flexibility for sorting.
  • Complete one color at a time: Focus on completing one color before moving to another.
  • Don't rush: Water Sort Puzzle is not a timed game. Take your time to consider all possible moves.
  • Use undo wisely: If you realize you've made a mistake, use the undo feature instead of trying to fix it with additional moves.

Difficulty Progression

As you advance through levels, you'll encounter:

  • More bottles: Higher levels will introduce more bottles to sort.
  • More colors: You'll need to manage an increasing number of different colors.
  • Complex arrangements: Colors will be dispersed in more challenging patterns.
  • Limited empty bottles: Fewer empty bottles means less room for maneuvering.
